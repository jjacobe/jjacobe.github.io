Target Audience

The target audience are aimed towards adults who enjoy outdoor activites in the Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven areas of Idaho.

Personas

Person 1

John Smith

John Smith is 28 years old and enjoys going hiking, biking, climbing, and camping. Whenever he has free time, he goes out to enjoy these activities with his friends. He checks the weather to see if it is good weather to go. He uses his phone to check the weather.

Scenarios

  • He checks for rain and thunderstorms in the area

  • He wants to know the temperature, humidity, sunrise and sunset times

  • He makes sure if he needs to bring a jacket if it's too cold

  • He looks up the forecast for the weekdays and weekends

  • He reads the weather news articles of any upcoming events

Person 2

Jessica Williams

Jessica Williams is 34 years old and works as a photographer. She is married with two kids and spends her off days with her family. She travels quite often for work to take outdoor shoots. With her kids, she takes them to soccer practice and watches their games on the weekends. She checks the weather on her computer often for her work and her kids games.

Scenarios

  • She checks for rain, overcast, and thunderstorms in the area for her work

  • She stays updated on the sunrise, peak sun, and sunset times for different shoots

  • She looks if she needs to bring a jacket or umbrella for her kids games

  • She wants to know the forecast for the weekend games of her kids

  • She reads weather news posts of any warnings in local areas
