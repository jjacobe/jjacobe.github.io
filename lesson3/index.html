Site Purpose

Forecast vibes provides the user with live, accurate weather as well as the forecast for the week. This user friendly site will present weather information for the Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven areas with a storm center and gallery. These town weather pages will show current weather conditions, relevant articles and content.
