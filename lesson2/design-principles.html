Design Principles

Proximity

Social Media | Instagram

The login page shows good use of proximity when trying to sign into an existing account or creating one. The text boxes are organized close together and easy to understand to what their corresponding function.

Instagram Link

Alignment

Travel | American Airlines

On their homepage, we can identify the alignment properties easily as their drop menu is evenly proportional and centered. Each box is spread out and well organized. Even their top navigation bar is aligned nicely.

American Airlines Link

Contrast

Electronics | Samsung

This homepage shows great use of contrast shown from the range of colors used. Their headings catch the viewers attention and is easy to read. Along with the button, you can see that it stands out well amongst the background.

Samsung Link
